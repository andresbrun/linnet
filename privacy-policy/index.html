Privacy

Private by default.

Linnet is designed to help with personal relationships without turning them into a source of surveillance. This page reflects the current behavior of the app and should stay aligned with the App Store privacy disclosure.

Summary

Linnet does not require an account. According to the current App Store privacy declaration for this app, data is not collected.

Information stored on your device

The app stores the relationships, events, reminders, and settings you create so the app can function. That information is meant to stay under your control on your device.

Optional platform integrations

  • Contacts access is requested only if you choose to import or link people from your contacts.
  • Notifications are used only if you enable reminder alerts.
  • iCloud sync may be used, when available, to keep your data in sync across your devices.

Data collection

The current App Store privacy disclosure for Linnet states that data is not collected. If this changes in a future version, this policy should be updated before that version is released.
