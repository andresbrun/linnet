A quieter workflow for showing up well.

Linnet sits between a birthday reminder app and a personal CRM. It is built for real life, where caring is easy and follow-through is the hard part.

1

Keep the right people close

Add friends, family, and partners once. Birthdays, milestones, follow-ups, and recurring rituals stay anchored to the relationship itself.

2

See moments before they become regrets

Birthdays are only one part of the picture. Linnet also helps with check-ins, anniversaries, traditions, recurring calls, and the small caring actions that keep people feeling remembered.

3

Turn reminders into thoughtful action

The app keeps enough context around each moment to help you act early and naturally, instead of scrambling at the last minute.

Designed to feel warm, not clinical.

Most alternatives split the problem the wrong way. Linnet keeps dates, people, and thoughtful follow-through together inside a private, consumer-friendly app.

What Linnet handles well

  • Birthdays, anniversaries, and yearly traditions
  • Recurring check-ins and follow-ups
  • People-centered reminders with real context
  • Share sheet capture and flexible scheduling
  • Private iCloud sync when available

What it deliberately avoids

  • No social feed
  • No sales-style CRM language
  • No gift-store clutter
  • No gamified couples-app mechanics
  • No account wall just to get started
