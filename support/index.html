Common questions

Do I need an account?

No. Linnet is designed to work without forcing sign-up first.

Why does Linnet ask for contacts access?

Only to help you import or link people if you choose to use that feature.

Why are reminders not appearing?

Check notification permissions in system settings and verify that the reminder has push enabled.

Does Linnet sync between devices?

It supports private iCloud sync when that service is available and enabled on your devices.
